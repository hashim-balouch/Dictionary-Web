Future Updates

I am excited to announce that I am working on a number of new features and improvements for my dictionary website. These updates will make my website more user-friendly, informative, and secure.

Upcoming features:

  • Profile system:

    I am adding a new profile system that will allow you to create and manage your own profile. You will be able to add your name, email address, and avatar, as well as follow other users and save your favorite words.

  • New UI:

    I am also developing a new user interface that is more modern and user-friendly. The new UI will make it easier for you to find the information you need, and it will be more accessible to users with disabilities.

  • Improved performance:

    I am working to improve the performance of my website so that it loads faster and responds more smoothly.

  • Enhanced security:

    I am constantly working to improve the security of my website to protect your data.

  • New features:

    I am also adding a number of new features to my website, including:

    • Word of the day:

      I will be sending you a new word every day, along with its definition, a usage example and other informationabout it.

    • Favorites and history:

      You will be able to save your favorite words and view a history of the words you have searched.

    • Sharing and copying:

      You will be able to share words and their definitions with your friends and family, or copy them to your clipboard.

  • Universal responsiveness:

    I am working to make my website universally responsive, so that it looks and functions great on all devices, including PCs, smartphones, and tablets. Currently, my website is responsive to PC screens and mobile devices whose screen-width is between 380-450 px. This covers approximately 80% of all commercial and other mobile devices.
